# Credits and Attributions

This project builds upon the excellent work of several projects and resources. We are grateful to the following projects and their contributors:

## Sudoku Generation and Solving

### QQwing - Sudoku of the Day Generation
- **Project**: [QQwing](https://github.com/stephenostermiller/qqwing)
- **Author**: Stephen Ostermiller
- **Usage**: Used for generating daily Sudoku puzzles
- **License**: [GPL-2.0](https://qqwing.com/about.html)
- **Description**: QQwing is a fast Sudoku generator and solver that produces high-quality, human-solvable puzzles with unique solutions.

### Tdoku - Sudoku Solver for Scanned Puzzles
- **Project**: [Tdoku](https://github.com/t-dillon/tdoku)
- **Author**: Tom Dillon
- **Usage**: Used for solving Sudoku puzzles captured through camera scanning
- **License**: [BSD 2-Clause License](https://github.com/t-dillon/tdoku/blob/master/LICENSE)
- **Description**: An extremely fast Sudoku solver optimized for solving puzzles with minimal clues and handling difficult cases.

## Augmented Reality Features

### AR Browser Sudoku
- **Project**: [AR Browser Sudoku](https://github.com/atomic14/ar-browser-sudoku)
- **Author**: Chris Greening (atomic14)
- **Usage**: Augmented reality scanner for capturing Sudoku puzzles from physical media
- **License**: [Creative Commons Zero v1.0 Universal](https://github.com/atomic14/ar-browser-sudoku/blob/master/LICENSE)
- **Description**: Browser-based augmented reality technology for detecting and extracting Sudoku grids from camera input using computer vision techniques.

## Difficulty Calculation

### Sudoku Coach - Difficulty Calculation Inspiration
- **Resource**: [Sudoku Coach Constructor](https://sudoku.coach/en/construct/)
- **Usage**: Inspiration for human-solver difficulty calculation algorithms used in generating puzzle difficulty ratings
- **Description**: The difficulty calculation system for the Sudoku book uses programmatically generated seeds with human solver difficulty inspired by the Sudoku Coach methodology, which analyzes solving techniques required to complete puzzles.
